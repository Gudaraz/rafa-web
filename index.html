Manual processing of intestate succession cases took 60 minutes per file, involving complex multi-field forms and external fiscal data lookups.

Implementation Strategy

Redesigned the end-to-end workflow, automating document generation for court submissions, deeds, and appeals.

Reduced processing time by 80% (60 to 15 minutes per case) and achieved 100% team adoption.
